F.A.Q.

  • What API's are being used on Book•Smart?

    Book•Smart uses the The New York Times Best Sellers API , Google Books API, and Open Library Books API.

    Why can I not find the book/author I am looking for?

    If you are unable to locate the book/author you are looking for chances are that the book/author is not in the Google Books API data.

    Why was Book•Smart made?

    Book•Smart was made to let users easily search for Books/Authors and to view it's data from The New York Times Best Sellers list.

    How far back in time does the data for New Yorks Times Best Sellers go?

    The data available from The New York Times API only goes back to 2009.
